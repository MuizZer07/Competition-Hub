1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

This Document will show a detailed description of our software called "Competition Hub". It will contain a step by step documentation of the software by which users and client can get an idea about how it should be operated in day-to-day life.

1.2 Document Conventions

We will use appropiate text size for readers of all ages. Texts color will be eye soothing. There will be bordered layouts to make the reading convenient.

1.3 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

This document is mainly for the audience who are involved in developing, designing, marketing and also who wants to invest. This document will show every details of the software.

1.4 Product Scope

The main purpose of this software is to connect the organizers and interested participants of any kind of competition or event. This will help to publish posters, reach distant audience, achieve global explosure for the organizers. Participants will be able to know all the details and check out all the information of any upcoming events.

Appendix A: Glossary

Competition Admin: Person who creates a competition, accepts participants, does all the organizing tasks
Participants: Person who participates in different competitions according to their choices. They must have an account with all his/her information and should fill up the required documents to participate
Viewers: Random viewrs who can view all the public contests, history, results or any kind of public information. They don't need any account to view

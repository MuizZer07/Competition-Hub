2. Overall Description

2.1 Product Perspective

We find many people struggling with finding a suitable competition or contest. Many of us often miss the type of competitions which are most suitable for us. So the main perspective of this product is to give someone the best idea of all the information which user needs to find thier suitable contest. A user can even get the privious seasons records of that contest(if any). If all the requirements are met then user can register for a competition. A user can also post a competition.

2.2 Product Functions

There will be two kinds of users; user who will post the competition (whom we will call organizer) and users who wants to participate in the competition (whom we will call participant).A participant can get access of competitions, where the competition will be held, see the current members of that competition, search for suitable competition. Basically, this product will help someone to find the perfect competion he/she wants. This product will prioritize all the option a participant looking for. For example, suppose someone looking for a competition related with robotics, he/she can also define thier level of knowleadge (beginner/intermediate/advanced), what should be the prize etc. So, our product will look for the best match and suggest the competition. participant can get the augmented view and decide if he/she wants to take it or not. A organizer can upload pictures, videos and obviously descriptions of his competition. He can specify any other requirements he might have like the competition is for which level of participant, what is the basic requirement. And can even create a form which a participant have to fill up in order to participate.

2.3 User Classes and Characteristics

There will be two kinds of users:
1. Organizer who want to participant to join in their competitions.
2. participants who are in search of a suitable competition.

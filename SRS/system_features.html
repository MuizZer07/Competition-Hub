4. System Features

Users, both competition organizers and participants will be able to enjoy the following features provided by admin

4.1 Competition Search

Users can perform search from large number of competitions by type and institution of the competition.

4.2 Create Competition

Competition organizers who are user, will have the authority to create competition and promote it.

4.3 Participant Filter

For the participants, there will be a form with question answers that will help to filter out interested and expected candidates and help organizer to estimate total number of participants

4.4 Smart suggestion

With help of AI we will send competition suggetions to participants on the basis of their interest.

1.0 Introduction

1.1 PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT

The purpose of this design document is to present the system design plan of the product named "CompetitionHub" by which anyone can discover any best events or competitions. Competitions will be made in our system by the coordinators of the event with the goal that individuals can locate a specific event and can influence their enrollment, to discover all the relative data and will get updates before the occasion begins. This Design Document will check that the present outline meets the majority of the express prerequisites contained in the genuine model and also the important necessities wanted by the user. It likewise intends to give a more point by point comprehension of the outline and design of the "CompetitionHub". Topics covered include the following:

  • Detailed description of components
  • User interface design
  • Architecture of the design
  • Processing narratives
  • Design constraints and restrictions
  • Test cases and expected results

1.2 SCOPE OF PROJECT

This software system will be a platform for those individuals who are occupied with taking an interest or sorting out competitions or events .This system will maximize the competition's productivity by providing platform of all types of competitions at one place, which would rather have to be done by manually finding one by one. Users will be encouraged with simple enrollment process. Also, will get a wide range of data and updates about the event. This will be an awesome promoting place for the coordinators who needs more competitors in their event.
